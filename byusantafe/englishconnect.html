WANT TO LEARN ENGLISH 100% FREE?

Whether you are interested in studying at BYU Pathway Worldwide or participating in PathwayConnect but do not know English or would simply like to learn English as a second language, English Connect will help you achieve this goal. It is divided into 3 levels: English Connect 1, for people who have no knowledge of the language and EnglishConnect 2 for people with a basic knowledge of the language, are taught in person, while EnglishConnect 3 for those who have a low intermediate level, It is developed in a 100% virtual way.

This program developed by the self-reliance services of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, helps anyone who wishes to learn English completely free of charge through face-to-face and virtual classes in the different church buildings. The study materials and course guidelines are verified and have a high success rate for students, teaching them English in an innovative way and preparing them to engage in fluent conversations in just a few months. If you are interested in English Connect, do not hesitate to contact us through our form in the "contact us" section.
