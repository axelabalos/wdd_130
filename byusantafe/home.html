READY TO GET YOUR 100% ONLINE DEGREE FROM SANTA FE?

BYU PathwayWorldwide, an online university of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, in association with BYU Idaho and Ensign College, has developed a program for those around the world with challenges similar to yours might be able to achieve their academic goals. To take part in this program, applicants participate in PATHWAYCONNECT, a preliminary leveling program where they gather the basic knowledge to be able to study at the university level. Because this program is provided by an American university, the whole content is in english so students need to know this language. If you do not know english, you can participate in ENGLISHCONNECT, where you can learn from zero or sthrenght your prior knowledge.

100% online

The costs of studying are beyond your income, you do not have a vehicle or the transport service is very expensive, or the course hours conflict with your other responsibilities? Through BYU Pathway Worldwide, you will now be able to obtain a bachelor's degree or lesser degrees at very low costs, studying at the times you have available and 100% online, so you will not have to spend money on transportation. You can study from Santa Fe, and earn an international degree.
