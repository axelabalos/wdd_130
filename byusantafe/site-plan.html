Overview

Purpose

BYU Pathway Worldwide is growing day by day, but there are too many people around the world who did not know about this amazing program still. By creating this website, we introduce Pathway Worldwide, PathwayConnect and English Connect programs to people in Santa Fe, Argentina. We will post information in spanish about the structure, benefits, cost and degrees of BYU Pathway.

Audience

This website is intended for those people in Santa Fe who want to know more about Pathway, its degree options, or want to know how to enroll if they do not know english. The material is explained in spanish so the people can understand better about the programs.

Content

Home page

Would you like to obtain a university degree but do not have the necessary means to achieve it? The costs of studying are beyond your income, you do not have a vehicle or the transport service is very expensive, or the course hours conflict with your other responsibilities? Through BYU Pathway Worldwide, you will now be able to obtain a bachelor's degree or lesser degrees at very low costs, studying at the times you have available and 100% online, so you will not have to spend money on transportation. You can study from Santa Fe, and earn an international degree. This university, of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, in association with BYU Idaho and Ensign College, has developed a program for those around the world with challenges similar to yours might be able to achieve their academic goals. To take part in this program, applicants participate in PATHWAYCONNECT, a preliminary leveling program where they gather the basic knowledge to be able to study at the university level. Because this program is provided by an American university, the whole content is in english so students need to know this language. If you do not know english, you can participate in ENGLISHCONNECT, where you can learn from zero or sthrenght your prior knowledge.

EnglishConnect

Whether you are interested in studying at BYU Pathway Worldwide or participating in PathwayConnect but do not know English or would simply like to learn English as a second language, English Connect will help you achieve this goal. This program developed by the self-sufficiency services of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, helps anyone who wishes to learn English completely free of charge through face-to-face and virtual classes in the different church buildings. The study materials and course guidelines are verified and have a high success rate for students, teaching them English in an innovative way and preparing them to engage in fluent conversations in just a few months. It is divided into 3 levels: English Connect 1, for people who have no knowledge of the language and EnglishConnect 2 for people with a basic knowledge of the language, are taught in person, while EnglishConnect 3 for those who have a low intermediate level, It is developed in a 100% virtual way. If you are interested in English Connect, do not hesitate to contact us through our form in the "contact us" section.

PathwayConnect

PathwayConnect will provide you with valuable tools for your life while preparing you to begin your studies at BYU Pathway Worldwide. Something important to keep in mind is that you do not need to have completed high school to be able to enter to study a university degree, since through PathwayConnect you will learn all the knowledge strictly necessary for your academic development. This 1-year program is divided into 3 four-month courses: 1) LIFE SKILLS, where some of the things you will learn will be lessons for your life, such as why study, how to manage your time, how to manage your finances, mathematics basic and writing lessons; 2) PROFESSIONAL SKILLS, you will be able to train yourself so that you can improve your job, you will learn to make contact networks, make a resume and advanced mathematics. 3) In the last quarter, UNIVERSITY SKILLS, you will be able to prepare to start your BYU PathwayWorldwide career, choosing what to study, completing the university entrance forms and all the other procedures related to admission, while learning soft skills for your mental well-being and emotional as a college student.
