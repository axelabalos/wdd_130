PREPARE FOR START A DEGREE

PathwayConnect will provide you with valuable tools for your life while preparing you to begin your studies at BYU Pathway Worldwide. Something important to keep in mind is that you do not need to have completed high school to be able to enter to study a university degree, since through PathwayConnect you will learn all the knowledge strictly necessary for your academic development.

This 1-year program is divided into 3 four-month courses: 1) LIFE SKILLS, where some of the things you will learn will be lessons for your life, such as why study, how to manage your time, how to manage your finances, mathematics basic and writing lessons; 2) PROFESSIONAL SKILLS, you will be able to train yourself so that you can improve your job, you will learn to make contact networks, make a resume and advanced mathematics. 3) In the last quarter, UNIVERSITY SKILLS, you will be able to prepare to start your BYU PathwayWorldwide career, choosing what to study, completing the university entrance forms and all the other procedures related to admission, while learning soft skills for your mental well-being and emotional as a college student.
